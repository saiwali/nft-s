NFT ( non-fungible token)





What is a NFT ??

A non-fungible token is a non-interchangeable unit of data stored on a blockchain, that can be sold and traded. NFT's can be photos, videos, and audio files etc.

How can be a NFT be used ?

--> NFTs can be used to represent real-world items."Tokenizing" these real-world tangible assets allows them to be bought, sold, and traded more efficiently while reducing the probability of fraud.

--> These NFT's provide a great oppourtunity for designers , artists etc.

How can be a NFT be used ?

--> mainly due to the invovlement of blockchain technology buying and selling NFT's helps people to make great deal of profit .

--> Some people invest in them because they believe that they'll hold value in the future, offering what they consider a safe investment option. Others invest in NFTs to secure digital ownership of physical assets and collectibles, as well as access to exclusive perks, since they are difficult to forge or steal.

What technology is nft's based on ??




Blockchain Technology .


A blockchain is essentially a digital ledger of transactions that is duplicated and distributed across the entire network of computer systems on the blockchain. The decentralised database managed by multiple participants is known as Distributed Ledger Technology (DLT).
